My name is Gintare, I am from Lithuania.
Since I was very young, I used to hear people talking about their
adventures in different countries, and I would always dream of
seeing these places with my own eyes. Growing up. I did not
have a chance to travel much, so now, as a young adult, I got
this chance to be independent and try to fulfill my wishes.

Why I enjoy traveling?
Yes, it takes a lot of time, money, and finally energy.
However, the feeling of experiencing a new cultures,
walking around the places you only saw on TV
before, is worth all of the struggle.
